My name is Hamdi Hamzeh, after doing freehand drawing and flash animation in highschool I went on to study animation where I graduated with a Bachelors degree in 2019. Since then I have been working on my programming, web development, art and creative skillset.

I have a wide set of skills, I like to work in both creative and technical fields such as creating animations and illustrations and writing code for websites and applications. I hope to further my skillset and work on more projects to boost my skills.

I speak Arabic and English fluently, I play guitar, videogames and draw. I hope to work on a videogame, release an album and write a book or a graphic novel.
